Menus

At Brio Catering, we offer two distinct food menus to suit your events unique style. Our Royal Banquet menu is designed for a full-service luxury dining experience, while our Gastronomy Luxe menu delivers a more contemporary twist on fine dining.

Additionally, our Shaken & Stirred cocktail menu can be enjoyed as a standalone service or paired seamlessly with either food menu, providing the perfect complement to any occasion.

Royal Banquet

The Royal Banquet is our premium catering package, designed for clients who want a full-service luxury dining experience.

Appetizers

Crown Jewel Canapés: Mini toasts with smoked salmon, dill crème fraîche, and lemon zest

Regal Prawn Cocktails: Tiger prawns with avocado mousse and spiced Marie Rose sauce

Main Course

Châteaubriand Royale: Prime beef tenderloin with red wine shallot reduction

Herb-Crusted Lamb Crown: Served with minted pea purée and rosemary jus

Seared Sea Bass Veronique: With champagne grapes and white wine beurre blanc

Wild Mushroom & Truffle Risotto (V): Creamy risotto with aged parmesan and black truffle oil

Desserts

Royal Chocolate Dome: Dark chocolate mousse, hazelnut praline center, gold dust

Lemon Lavender Tartlets: With candied violets and mascarpone cream

Eton Mess à la Royale: Berries, crushed meringue, Chantilly cream, edible petals

Rates

Our preferred rates for the Royal Banquet menu start at €100.00 per guest, with packages available for events of all sizes.

10% discount applies to events of over 150 guests.

Custom pricing arrangements can be discussed upon intitial booking.
